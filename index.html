What is a Cell?

Cells are the structural and functional unit of life. Organisms are unicellular or multicellular.

Your body hosts ~37 trillion cells; ~3 million die & are replaced each second.

Cell Theory

  • All organisms are made of one or more cells.
  • The cell is the basic unit of structure & function.
  • Cells arise from pre-existing cells.

Plant Cell — The Green Powerhouse

Labeled Plant Cell

Key parts: cell wall, plasma membrane, cytoplasm, nucleus, chloroplasts, large central vacuole, mitochondria, ER, Golgi, ribosomes, plasmodesmata.

Thylakoids (stacked as grana) in stroma; light rxns on thylakoids, carbon fixation in stroma.
